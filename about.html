01. ABOUT

I am a second-year Computer Science student at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune (VIT Pune).

I believe in skill more than grades. My philosophy is rooted in the grind mentality—a commitment to continuous improvement, deep technical understanding, and the relentless pursuit of engineering mastery. For me, engineering isn't just a career; it's a discipline inspired by the complexity and logic seen in the technical world and its portrayal in media.

Motivation

Inspired by pioneers like Kevin Mitnick and Edward Snowden, I view the "grind" as a journey of sitting for hours, dedicated to understanding systems and securing them against vulnerabilities.

The Environment

I thrive in a high-discipline environment. A spotless, organized desk is non-negotiable for my workflow. Paired with serious, focused music, it creates the ideal space for deep work.

Current Focus & Vision

Currently, I am diving deep into Ethical Hacking and Network Security. My ultimate goal is to apply my skills as a Cybersecurity Specialist to make the digital world safer and more resilient.

I am working towards a future in a tech-rich, secure, and innovative environment—aiming for global hubs where cybersecurity excellence is a standard.
